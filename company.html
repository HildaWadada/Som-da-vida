About Us

Som da Vida is more than a company — it's a cultural force. We are a music and management brand built to empower artists, elevate live experiences, and amplify the sound of life across borders.

Rooted in rhythm and driven by purpose, we specialize in developing raw talent and curating unforgettable event experiences. Whether on stage, behind the scenes, or in the studio — our mission is to connect people through the power of sound.

Mission

To manage and elevate music with integrity, creativity, and soul — turning passion into purpose and sound into legacy.

Vision

To become a global movement that nurtures music, celebrates culture, and transforms lives through the power of rhythm.

Core Values

  • Creativity — We push boundaries, think boldly, and give space for unique expression across every part of our brand.
  • Authenticity — Real people. Real stories. Real sound. We uplift truth and talent that comes straight from the soul.
  • Collaboration — From artists to audience, we believe in building together. Our success is shared.
  • Culture-First — We don’t follow trends. We move with culture, shape the vibe, and represent the roots.

Our Services

Artist Management
We guide talent from the ground up — from studio to stage. We offer strategic support in music production, brand growth, and bookings.

Event Management
We design and execute events that bring energy, culture, and unforgettable memories to life — locally and internationally.

Creative Direction & Branding
From visuals to storytelling, we help artists and brands shape a unique identity that speaks loud and clear.
